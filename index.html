Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 22, 2025

1. Overview

This extension works entirely on your device. It allows you to:

  • Track time spent on projects
  • Create and manage reminders

2. Data Collected

The extension does not collect or transmit any personal or usage data. All information is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

3. Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s internal chrome.storage.local API to save:

  • Reminders
  • Project names
  • Time tracking entries
  • Selected active project

This information never leaves your device.

4. How Your Data Is Used

Your locally stored data is used exclusively to:

  • Display reminders and tasks
  • Track time for selected projects
  • Schedule and trigger reminder notifications via alarm

No data is transmitted to any remote server.

5. Permissions

The extension uses the following permissions:

  • storage — to save reminders and time entries
  • alarms — to schedule reminder triggers
  • notifications — to display reminder notifications

No additional permissions are requested.

6. Data Sharing

The extension does not share data with any external party, and it does not use analytics, advertisements, tracking, or third-party services.

7. Data Security

All stored information remains on your device and is protected by your browser's built-in security features.

8. Data Retention

Data remains stored locally until you delete it manually or uninstall the extension. Uninstalling the extension permanently removes all locally stored data.

9. Cookies

This extension does not use cookies. It does not create, read, or store cookies, nor does it use third-party cookies or tracking technologies.

10. Your Control

You may view, edit, or delete your reminders and time entries at any time within the extension. Removing the extension deletes all stored data.

11. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date above.
